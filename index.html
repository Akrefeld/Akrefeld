Hi — welcome to my homepage.

I am a researcher in Marketing and Assistant Professor at Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University. My research combines cognitive science and marketing to better understand consumer behaviour, managerial decision making, and interventions for sustainability.

I focus on sustainable consumer behaviour, persuasive communication, and methodological improvements in consumer research. Recent work also addresses climate risk communication and behaviour change interventions.
