Hello, welcome to my homepage.

I am a researcher in Marketing and Assistant Professor at Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University. My research combines cognitive science and marketing to better understand consumer behavior, managerial decision making, and interventions for sustainability.

My work focuses on sustainable consumer behavior, persuasive communication, and methodological improvements in consumer research. I am especially interested in how research can contribute to climate change adaptation and mitigation.
